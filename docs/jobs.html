Two quick things to know

The Hub job board is built to help you support your clients faster. Here's how to use it:

⭐

Want to submit a job to the board?

If you've come across a great opportunity, drop it here. We review submissions and add them to the next weekly list, marked with a star so others know it came from a counsellor.

Click here!
✉

Want to send a job to a client?

Every job card has a Send to client button that opens your email with the job details, apply link, and personalized resume tips pre-filled. Just add the client's name and send.
