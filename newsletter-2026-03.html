Unlocking Better Practice - Spring Sessions from theHUB @PATH

Kevin Westwood Wednesday, March 25, 2026

Letter from Kevin

Over the past year, it has been a genuine privilege to be part of the continued growth of our service provider network. The willingness across the network to explore new approaches, strengthen practice, and support one another has been both encouraging and impactful.

This year alone, we've had the opportunity to support 426 consultations across the network, with the vast majority of participants sharing that these conversations were extremely useful in their day-to-day practice. That level of engagement and openness is what makes this work meaningful.

Thank you for trusting theHUB @PATH to come alongside your teams.

As we continue to evolve and refine our services, your perspective remains incredibly important to us. We would greatly value any feedback you or your teams are willing to share:

theHUB @PATH 2025-2026 Year End Feedback Survey
