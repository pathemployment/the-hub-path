March 2026 Newsletter

Unlocking Better Practice — Spring Sessions from theHUB @PATH

Kevin Westwood Wednesday, March 25, 2026

Letter from Kevin

Over the past year, it has been a genuine privilege to be part of the continued growth of our service provider network. The willingness across the network to explore new approaches, strengthen practice, and support one another has been both encouraging and impactful.

This year alone, we've had the opportunity to support 426 consultations across the network, with the vast majority of participants sharing that these conversations were extremely useful in their day-to-day practice. That level of engagement and openness is what makes this work meaningful.

Thank you for trusting theHUB @PATH to come alongside your teams. Being invited into your organizations and having the opportunity to support your staff and clients is something we do not take lightly.

As we continue to evolve and refine our services, your perspective remains incredibly important to us. We would greatly value any feedback you or your teams are willing to share. To support this, we have included a feedback survey where you can provide your thoughts and experiences:

theHUB @PATH 2025–2026 Year End Feedback Survey

As you dive into this newsletter you will notice a couple of new series have started. theHUB Essentials is a great series for anyone who doesn't know exactly what we do. You will learn about how we can come alongside you in your work to support clients. Breaking Barriers will be looking at particular barriers and how we can break them, allow our clients to be appropriately strengthened, and accommodated.

We look forward to continuing this work together.

theHUB Essentials — Career Services Consultant (CSC) Webinar

Mateja Mihelcic

Ever wondered what a Career Services Consultant actually does? Join us for a "look under the hood" webinar around what theHUB @PATH can add to your daily practice as an Employment Professional.

Whether you've never accessed any resources before and you're curious to learn about what theHUB has to offer — or have been using our services and want to delve into how to communicate results around assessments, or the dreaded "what now?" when new information comes to light — this session is for you.

Join us for a webinar that takes a deep look into what to expect when you do a consultation, provides clear delineation of who and where to access services from, what makes an appropriate candidate for consultation, and the types of tools we employ to help you better serve your clients with barriers within our peer-to-peer model.

June Professional Development — Breaking Barriers: Supporting Deaf and Hard of Hearing Job Seekers

Shannon McCracken

This session is designed for employment service providers, career practitioners, and employers who want to strengthen their approach to inclusive, accessible employment support. Grounded in leading insights from Deaf studies and practical, real-world strategies, this professional development session will explore:

  • Key cultural and communication considerations within the Deaf community
  • Common barriers faced by Deaf and Hard of Hearing job seekers
  • Practical strategies to enhance accessibility in your services
  • How to support employers in creating inclusive hiring and workplace environments

Whether you're building foundational knowledge or looking to deepen your practice, you'll leave with actionable tools you can apply immediately. Join us in strengthening inclusive employment practices and creating more equitable opportunities for all job seekers.
